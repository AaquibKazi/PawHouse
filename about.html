Our Mission

It is the creation of Muskan Bhalla, Amal Amzath, Imthiyaz Najeeb, Aaquib Kazi, students of JSSIS.
We are presenting a platform to show our love and care for the lovly creatures who don't have a home, family and words to express themselves.
Somewhere they are looking for a home and support from those who understands their feelings.
If you find it interesting, let us introduce ourselves, we are young and energetic team working on this interesting theme to collect needs of someone with support of all of you.
